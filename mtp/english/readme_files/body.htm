1. Components

- - - Mazzy the Paladin
Mazzy gets a quest, involving the possibility of becoming a paladin. It isn't still complete, since all the banters and other dialogue of Mazzy still think that she is the usual BioWare figher.
Q: Why should I use this mod instead of the excellent Level 1 NPCs provided by GB?
A: Three reasons. The first is that I provide a quest, full with with new fights and colourful encounters, while GB flat-changes the class. Second, mine is a WeiDU install, and as such I won't overwrite anything important. Third, Mazzy's weapons get improved two times, so now they make sense.
Q: Why should I use the excellent Level 1 NPCs provided by GB instead of this mod?
A: Three reasons. First: GB's mod provides similar choiches for almost all BioWare NPCs. Second: they are level 1, so that you can choose their weapons Proficiencies as fit. Third: they are in their new class from when you get them, not after a quest.


2. Installation and uninstallation

It's not the same as most WeiDU mods, since you have to check some stuff manually!
You should obtain and install the latest SOA or TOB patch from Interplay or Bioware BEFORE installing any of these components.
After that, get the last Baldurash post-patch Fix Pack from Baldurdash

3. Known bugs and incompatibility problems

(In)Compatibility:

Mazzy
Some scripts in the mod require that Detectable Spells is installed in order to work properly. You can get somewhat near here Cirrerek's main page. The mod will install properly also without it, but it won't run at 100%. Please also note that many mods (to name a few: Ascension, Kelsey, Kiara/Zayia, Spell 50, Oversight - Tougher Sendai) already provide Detectable spells, so 90% of the times this won't be a major problem.
Alas, this would mean that the Stupid Script of Dissaster (you know what I mean, eheheh) won't run properly. Another good reason to uninstall it.
Mazzy
I experienced some problems with the kit-changing code which is causated by Oversight - cleric kits bad coding. I try to fix stuff on my own part, though.

Known Bugs or other in-game problems:

Mazzy
If you use the anti-undead scroll before talking to the lich, he won't talk to you. Cause: the scroll makes you immune to him. Solution: use the scroll after talking, or eventually use it on everybody but one party member, who will use it after having done the chitchat.

If you smell something wrong with another mod, or post at my forum, and I will do anything possible to fix it, or will warn people in the readme.
